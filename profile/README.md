## What We Do

We're a small studio based in **France** building two things we love: **games** and **AI-powered tools**.

Our MCP ([Model Context Protocol](https://modelcontextprotocol.io)) servers let AI assistants like **Claude**, **Cursor**, and **Windsurf** work directly inside the software you use every day — from **Unity Editor** to **Blender**, **Discord**, **Jira**, **GitHub**, **LinkedIn**, and more.
